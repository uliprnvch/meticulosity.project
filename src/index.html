About Us

The Un-Agency

What does it mean to be an "un-agency?" Well, we've heard what it's like working with the other guys, and while we provide many of the same services as traditional marketing, advertising, and development agencies, we don't work like they do.
Meticulosity was born digital. Our roots were in development and integration projects with some of the world's biggest and best-know e-commerce brands. Marketing came later, and we fell in love with the strategic, technical, and process parts of the Inbound flywheel.
